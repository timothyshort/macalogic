Information Assurance / Cyber Security

Position Title: Information Assurance / Cyber Security

Requisition Number: G-0417-104

Company Overview

Macalogic is a dynamic and growing company with broad competencies based on over a decade of experience working in the federal arena. We are an SBA-certified 8(a) and Economically Disadvantaged Woman-Owned Small Business (EDWOSB). We provide IT technical, program management, and business consulting services to customers in the Government and Commercial sectors.

Our core values of accountability, collaboration, value, and respect are central to everything we do at Macalogic. As such, we are always open to talking to self-motivated and responsible team players who are focused on contributing to the success of our clients. We offer a generous benefits package and compensation commensurate with your experience and skillset.

General Description

The successful candidate will be part of a team working on a government project to provide application hosting and migration expertise with special emphasis on cyber security and information assurance. This position will require detailed knowledge related to hardware, network, operating system software and DISA infrastructure components associated with a multi-node implementation of the Oracle eBusiness Suite.

The project is envisioned to address current cyber security and information assurance challenges through the acquisition of an integrated set of tools, services, and capabilities supporting the needs of the United States Air Force and United States Transportation Command financial communities.

Duties and Responsibilities
  • The successful candidate has strong knowledge of how the Government addresses the definition and implementation of IPs and host names; the definition and configuration of ports, protocols, and firewall components; and the integration of large scale applications with DISA’s F5 implementation and configuration.
  • The candidate should have knowledge of information assurance in a DoD/USAF environment. This includes running DISA STIGs and SCANs and/or evaluating results and recommending hardware/software/operating system modifications to address identified vulnerabilities.

Specialized Knowledge
  • Significant breadth and depth of knowledge and experience with Sun platforms, Solaris operating systems, Windows platforms and the Windows operating system.
  • Knowledge and experience supporting all facets of information assurance, the development of DIACAP/RMF documentation packages, and marshalling documentation through to the granting of system ATOs and ATCs.
  • This individual must be highly-motivated and able to work with little management directions.

Qualifications
Education Bachelor’s Degree in computer science or related field
Professional Certifications None
Clearance Either currently hold or ability to acquire a U.S. Security Clearance.
Experience Seven years of practical experience with large scale COTS-based applications; Federal government support, especially Air Force and/or Dept. of Defense related is a plus.
US Citizen or Similar Must be a U.S. Citizen
Skills and Abilities Problem Solving, Teamwork
Other Characteristics Excellent oral, written, and communication in English. Must be able to work in a team environment with members that include personnel from other companies, Government program management and administrative personnel, and technical staff members. This position may require travel of up to one week per month.
Working Conditions, Location

Onsite at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Dayton, OH. Client duty hours Monday through Friday.

Senior Business Analyst

Position Title: Senior Business Analyst

Requisition Number: G-0717-105

Location: Montgomery, AL

Company Overview

Macalogic is a dynamic and growing company with broad competencies based on over a decade of experience working in the federal arena. We are an SBA-certified 8(a) and Economically Disadvantaged Woman-Owned Small Business (EDWOSB). We provide IT technical, program management, and business consulting services to customers in the Government and Commercial sectors.

Our core values of accountability, collaboration, value, and respect are central to everything we do at Macalogic. As such, we are always open to talking to self-motivated and responsible team players who are focused on contributing to the success of our clients. We offer a generous benefits package and compensation commensurate with your experience and skillset.

General Description

The successful candidate will provide senior technical services to support the Core Financial Systems (CFS) suite of systems using a variety of Unisys products and provide performance analysis and capacity planning to include coordination with Unisys as needed for problem resolution.

Duties and Responsibilities

Provide senior functional and technical services required to maintain the CFS operational and developmental environments resulting from: hardware and software upgrades; technology/data migrations directed to maintain current technologies; vendor software changes; and course of action modifications to implement changes to Laws, Regulations and Policies (LRP). This individual will be responsible for functional trouble-shooting and problem resolution as well as first-line interaction with users of the GAFS-DTS system while also performing similar duties in a back-up role for the WinGAMPS/WinMOOPS system. In addition, the candidate will participate in: requirements analysis of government Configuration Control Board (CCB) approved Deficiency Reports (DRs) and System Change Requests (SCRs); Business Process Directory (BPD) System Requirements Reviews; Joint Application Design (JAD) sessions; Peer Reviews; formal Systems Engineering Reviews; and Test Readiness Reviews.

Specialized Knowledge
  • Experience in Unisys software development
  • Government BES BPD waterfall development methodology
  • System Technical knowledge/experience working with G13
  • Understanding of the travel process including the processing of orders/obligations, receipts and payments, and how the CDS system supports travel reimbursements

Qualifications
Education BS in Information Systems or equivalent
Professional Certifications Requires achievement of Security + CE
Clearance Either currently hold or ability to acquire a U.S. Security Clearance.
Experience Minimum of 5 years working with Unisys
US Citizen or Similar Must be a U.S. Citizen.
Skills and Abilities Excellent oral, written, and communication in English. Must be able to work in a team environment with members that include personnel from other companies, Government program management and administrative personnel, and technical staff members.
Working Conditions, Location

Onsite at Maxwell AFB-Gunter Annex in Montgomery, AL. Client duty hours Monday through Friday.

This position may require some travel.
