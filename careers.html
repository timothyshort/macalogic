Senior Data Modeler, Senior Systems Analyst

Position Title: Senior Data Modeler, Senior Systems Analyst

Requisition Number: G-1017-113

Quantico, VA

Company Overview

Macalogic is a dynamic and growing company with broad competencies based on over a decade of experience working in the federal arena. We are an SBA-certified 8(a) and Economically Disadvantaged Woman-Owned Small Business (EDWOSB). We provide IT technical, program management, and business consulting services to customers in the Government and Commercial sectors.

Our core values of accountability, collaboration, value, and respect are central to everything we do at Macalogic. As such, we are always open to talking to self-motivated and responsible team players who are focused on contributing to the success of our clients. We offer a generous benefits package and compensation commensurate with your experience and skillset.

General Description

Macalogic is seeking an experienced Data Modeler to work at Marine Corp Base (MCB) Quantico, Quantico, Virginia to provide data modeling and data architecture support. The candidate will perform in a lead Data Base Modeling (DBM) role providing support to system modernization efforts. This will include performing database requirements identification and development, developing data dictionaries, developing conceptual, logical and physical database models, and developing plans for legacy data clean-up, data transformation, and data conversion.

Duties and Responsibilities
  • Iteratively refine the existing enterprise conceptual data model,
  • Support and facilitate workshops and interviews to elicit information needed to develop logical and physical data models,
  • Develop the enterprise logical data model, including entities, attributes, and relationships,
  • Develop the enterprise physical data model, including tables, columns, and relationships,
  • Develop data dictionaries with agreed-upon definitions for all entities and attributes,
  • Map logical/physical attributes/columns to existing IT systems, identify gaps where data in existing systems do not exist to support the Target Data Architecture (TDA), and identify transformations where data must be altered from the source systems to be accommodated in the TDA.,
  • Architect and develop an overall master data hub for data elements that are used by multiple IT systems.

Specialized Knowledge
  • Conceptual data modeling,
  • Logical data modeling,
  • Physical data modeling,
  • Prior work and familiarity with databases (preferably Oracle, SQL Server, and Progress),
  • Data analysis,
  • Workshop and interview experience,
  • ERwin modeling tool experience and/or knowledge of and experience with program design and development with data interfaces including JDBC/ODBC, APIs, and data timestamping strategies,
  • Experience with data interrogation tools.

Qualifications
Education Bachelor’s Degree in a technical field and 3 years of experience.
Professional Certifications Security+
Clearance Either currently hold or ability to acquire a U.S. Security Clearance.
Experience 3 years of experience in data modeling.
US Citizen or Similar Must be a U.S. Citizen
Skills and Abilities Excellent oral, written, and communication in English. Must be able to work in a team environment with members that include personnel from other companies, Government program management and administrative personnel, and technical staff members. This position may require some travel.
Working Conditions, Location

Onsite in Quantico, VA. Client duty hours Monday through Friday.

Requisition Number: G-1117-124

Dayton, OH & Montgomery, AL

Company Overview

We are an SBA-certified 8(a) and Economically Disadvantaged Woman-Owned Small Business (EDWOSB). We provide IT technical, program management, and business consulting services to customers in the Government and Commercial sectors.

Our core values of accountability, collaboration, value, and respect are central to everything we do at Macalogic. As such, we are always open to talking to self-motivated and responsible team players who are focused on contributing to the success of our clients. We offer a generous benefits package and compensation commensurate with your experience and skillset.

General Description

These projects involve the migration of legacy business system applications from existing Government hosting environments to Amazon’s AWS or Microsoft Azure. This will include the development of all relevant project plans, the implementation of a structured program management and systems engineering process, the implementation of Agile software development principles, and the coordination and collaborate implementation of all activities across stakeholder groups such as Government PMOs, functional user communities, DISA (current hosting environment), AWS or Azure (target hosting environment), multiple test organizations, the Government Capabilities Integration Environment (CIE), and multiple security groups.

Duties and Responsibilities

The successful candidate will be responsible or the development and implementation of a data integration strategy, allowing the exchange of data between the AWS/Azure hosting environment and the data integration tools available via GCSS-AF. This includes the use of the DISA Enterprise Service Bus (ESB), S3, SQS, ActiveMQ Containers using Beanstalk, JMS Messaging, elastic cashing etc. In addition, the candidate will negotiate data exchange protocols with data exchange partners and DISA, perform MOA updates, and coordinate these changes with the system PMO and all trading partner systems.

Specialized Knowledge
  • Experience with performing application integration with technologies such as the DISA ESB, S3, SQS, ActiveMQ using Beanstalk, JMS Messaging,
  • Development of MOAs, MOUs, etc., and
  • Experience with PL/SQL
Qualifications
Education Bachelor’s Degree in computer science, management information systems or related field of study.
Professional Certifications Security+
Clearance Either currently hold or ability to acquire a U.S. Security Clearance.
Experience 5-10 years’ experience with data exchange in the Government environment
US Citizen or Similar Must be a U.S. Citizen
Skills and Abilities Experience with application migration

Excellent oral, written, and communication in English. Must be able to work in a team environment with members that include personnel from other companies, Government program management and administrative personnel, and technical staff members. This position may require some travel.
