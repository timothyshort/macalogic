Our mission is to improve our clients' business through the intelligent application of technology.

Macalogic is a dynamic and growing young company with broad competencies based on our founder’s decades of experience working in the federal arena. We are an SBA-certified 8(a), Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB), and Small Disadvantaged Business (SDB). Headquartered in Dayton, OH, we are also both MBE and EDGE certified.

Core
Values

Behind everything we do - every decision, every conversation, and every business relationship - we hold strongly to the convictions embodied in our four Core Values.

Accountability

We take personal responsibility for our work.

Value

We focus relentlessly on quality delivery to bring maximum value to our clients, partners, and peers.

Collaboration

We are effective and mindful communicators. We listen to truly understand priorities and focus on meeting common goals on every task, every time.

Respect

We appreciate different points of view, recognizing the importance of unique qualities and abilities. We respect ourselves, our peers, our clients, and our communities.
