Public Sector

Our focus on process enables us to help our clients meet their mission needs on large or small projects, because our processes are scalable, repeatable, and proven.

World Class Team Experience

Our team has worked with various government organizations and government sponsored enterprises on both the federal and state level.

Our senior leaders have decades of experience providing IT program management, acquisition support, process design and implementation, and training support. The majority of our work has been with Department of Defense organizations, but we also bring commercial and State experience.

We have broad experience in information technology, enterprise solutions, and infrastructure technologies. Our program support and administrative management teams include experienced practitioners who know how to apply DoD 5000.2 to Information System acquisitions.
