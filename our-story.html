About Us

Learn more about our story.

Macalogic is a dynamic and growing company with broad competencies based on over a decade of experience working in the federal arena. We are an SBA-certified 8(a), Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB), and Small Disadvantaged Business (SDB). Headquartered in Dayton, OH, we are also both MBE and EDGE certified. We provide IT technical and business consulting services to customers in the Government and Commercial sectors.

Whether you are looking for IT Infrastructure Support, Acquisition Management, Enterprise Systems, Modeling/Simulation Analysis, or assistance in managing Technology Projects, we can help you to be successful. Our certified and experienced staff can design, implement and support solutions based on technologies from Barracuda Networks, Cisco, Dell, Microsoft, VMWare, Websense, and other leading vendors.

Contact us to see how we can work together to meet your project needs.
