SeaPort-e

Macalogic was awarded a SeaPort-e contract in August 2016 under SeaPort-e’s most recent Rolling Admissions acquisition

About SeaPort-e

Available to support NAVSEA, NAVAIR, SPAWAR, NAVSUP, MSC, NAVFAC, SSP, ONR, and USMC

Support services available in 22 functional service areas, including Acquisition, Logistics, and Program and Financial Management

Benefits of using
SeaPort-e

SeaPort-e offers Significant Cost Reduction and Lower Acquisition Costs.

Automated processes for acquiring the services you need with decreased administration – All administrative interaction takes place on SeaPort.


Single Point of Administration - You do everything in SeaPort, from drafting a Statement of Work, to submitting a request for funding, to reviewing proposals, to awarding and administering the contract.


Evaluate and award in real-time - Contractor information and feedback from a NAVSEA Contracting Officer are immediately available; you always have the latest pre-award status.

Macalogic SeaPort-e Quality Assurance Approach

Macalogic embeds quality assurance in every project, from inception to closeout. The primary methods of assuring the quality of services are appropriate advance planning, assignment of sufficient qualified staff resources, provision of appropriate tools, and establishment of rigorous program and project management techniques.

Our Approach:


Establishing and monitoring metrics to evaluate specific project objectives


Establishing quality management processes that include monitoring and assessing performance


Coordinating with the government stakeholders to establish quality objectives by deciding the scope and frequency of quality assessments and reporting policies for each program we support


Including all members of our project teams in our rigorous quality program

TM Capture Services

TM Capture Services (TMCS), is is a dynamic and growing company with broad capability for providing technical and functional services to public and private sector clients. An SBA-certified 8(a) and Economically Disadvantaged Woman-Owned Small Business (EDWOSB) headquartered in Dayton, OH (Zone 5), TMCS focuses on providing practical, affordable solutions designed specifically to meet our clients’ business objectives. Our approach to applying technology to business needs includes an emphasis on outcome-centered activities that ensures our clients’ needs are fully addressed throughout every engagement. TMCS offers a broad array of IT and acquisition-related services, including program, project, and acquisition management, advisory and assistance services, data and configuration management, and document conversion. Our services align with the following SeaPort-e functional areas:

  • System Design Documentation and Technical Data Support
  • Software Engineering, Development, Programming, and Network Support
  • Configuration Management (CM) Support Information System (IS) Development, Information Assurance (IA) and Information Technology (IT) Support
  • Program Support
  • Functional and Administrative Support

Nova Technology Solutions

NovaTech is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) whose core business expertise focuses on the management and performance of information security, software engineering, information technology, acquisition program management and administration. NovaTech has provided information security, IT and acquisition management support services for over 12 years and boasts highly relevant experience in taking projects from a pre-production/production status and then providing installation, logistics, training, and provisioning support for the life cycle of the system. NovaTech offers depth of experience in the following SeaPort-e functional areas:

  • Engineering, System Engineering and Process Engineering Support
  • System Design Documentation and Technical Data Support
  • Software Engineering, Development, Programming, and Network Support
  • Configuration Management (CM) Support
  • Information System (IS) Development, Information Assurance (IA) and Information Technology (IT) Support
  • Program Support
  • Functional and Administrative Support
