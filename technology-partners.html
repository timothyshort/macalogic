Technology Partners

We have established partnering relationships with leading IT hardware and software manufacturers and service providers

Arcserve is a leading provider of data protection and recovery software that provides organizations with the assurance that they can recover their data and applications when needed. Macalogic uses Arcserve’s products to develop comprehensive on-premise backup and recovery solutions.
As a Certified Barracuda Networks Partner, Macalogic can provide world-class security, backup and IP Telephony solutions using Barracuda Networks’ suite of products. Barracuda Networks’ product portfolio includes the Barracuda Backup Server, Barracuda Spam Firewall, Barracuda Web Filter, Barracuda Message Archiver and the Barracuda Phone System.
CablesToGo is a leading manufacturer and distributor of computer cables and connectivity solutions. CablesToGo uses only the finest components and the latest construction technologies to optimize cable performance and ensure compliance to industry specifications for each cable and its designated application.
As a Cisco Select Partner, Macalogic can provide the latest switching, routing, wireless and firewall technologies from the worldwide leader in networking. Macalogic can develop solutions to provide our customers with a powerful communications platform that will serve as the basis for the convergence of data, voice, video and mobile communications in a secure, integrated architecture.
Crucial is a global brand of Micron Technology, Inc., one of the largest memory and flash storage manufacturers in the world. Macalogic can recommend computer memory upgrades (DRAM) and solid state drives (SSDs), and offer more than 250,000 upgrades for over 50,000 systems.
As a Dell Reseller, Macalogic can deliver the full range of Dell’s expansive product lines including desktop personal computers (PC), servers and networking products, storage, mobility products, software, peripherals and services. With Dell’s flexible design options, Macalogic can customize systems to meet individual customer requirements, install them at your location and provide ongoing system support.
ESET’s flagship product is Antivirus Endpoint which is built on the award-winning ThreatSense engine. ESET Antivirus Endpoint is a tightly integrated security solution that users can trust to protect their computers. As an ESET Affiliate, Macalogic can provide a comprehensive anti-virus solution for PC workstations, servers and e-mail servers using the award-winning ESET product suite.
Ingram Micro Inc. is the world’s largest technology distributor and a leading technology sales, marketing and logistics company. In partnership with Ingram Micro, Macalogic can offer our customers a broad array of solutions and IT products from nearly 1,400 suppliers and manufacturers.
In addition to being a Silver Microsoft Partner, Macalogic has been awarded the Midmarket Solution Provider and Desktop specializations. This means that our certified and experienced staff can install, configure, and support Microsoft products for small/medium businesses as well as large enterprises. As an authorized Microsoft reseller, N ovaTech can explain Microsoft’s complex licensing structure and help make sure our customers are getting maximum value out of their investments in Microsoft software.
Macalogic can use RingCentral’s offerings to deliver cloud business communications solutions that free people to work the way they want in today’s mobile, distributed and always-on work world. RingCentral enables convenient and effective communications across all locations, all employees, all the time, thus fostering a more productive and dynamic workforce.
Tandberg Data is a leading global supplier and manufacturer of backup and archiving solutions using tape drives, autoloaders, libraries, media, disk-based solutions, software and hardware components for mainstream configurations. As a Registered Tandberg Data Reseller, Macalogic can meet the growing storage requirements of small and medium sized organizations with scalable and reliable solutions that ensure cost effective operation and long-term investment protection.
As a VMware Professional Solution Provder, Macalogic can design, implement, and support vitualization solutions that answer the challenges of server consolidation and infrastructure optimization, business continuity, software life cycle automation and enterprise desktop management
Websense products increase employee Internet productivity and secure organizations from emerging Internet threats by providing a proactive critical security component that complements traditional security solutions. Macalogic can deploy Websense products that integrate with a wide range of industry leading security and network products including firewalls, proxy servers, caches, switches, routers, and appliances.
