Contracts

Macalogic’s professional and technical services are available today under multiple Government-wide Acquisition Contracts and Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contracts. Macalogic is committed to maintaining contract vehicles that offer our government customers the convenience, flexibility, and speed they require to meet the business and technical challenges they face in today’s fast-changing, budget constrained environment.

USN SeaPort-e

SeaPort-e is an IDIQ contract initially awarded in December 2008. Macalogic was awarded a contract under the 2016 Rolling Admissions solicitation. Services provided under this contract are categorized into 22 functional services areas. Our SeaPort-e contract was awarded for Zones 2, 4 and 5. The Macalogic Team includes AOC Total Solutions, LLC and TM Captures Services, LLC. This BPA is available to all NAVSEA, NAVAIR, SPAWAR, NAVSUP, MSC, NAVFAC, SSP, ONR, and USMC organizations. Macalogic teammates are ready to support SeaPort-e contracts, click here for more information.

GSA IT-70

Our GSA Schedule period of performance is from May 16, 2012 through May 15, 2017. The IT Support Schedule enables Macalogic to provide IT support services and help desk support to any federal agency and organization throughout the CONUS. Support services provided by Macalogic under this schedule include, but are not limited to, information systems architecture strategy, project management, systems analysis and design, web design and development, network analysis, setup and maintenance services, office automation support, data management, systems development, systems assurance, and systems implementation and operation.

USAF F2AST

The Future Flexible Acquisition & Sustainment Tool (F2AST) Program is a 5 year, Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (ID/IQ) contract awarded in July 2008. This contract provides development, modifications, and depot maintenance. It may include one-time spares and repairs which qualify as critical, limited, and/or contingency and sole source services to a F2AST contractor or large business subcontractor, such as other aircraft parts and auxiliary equipment manufacturing. As part of L3’s team, Macalogic can provide these services. This BPA is available to Air Force maintenance and depot organizations.

USAF NetCents II

Network-Centric Solutions (NETCENTS) is a 5 year, $9B Air Force Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (ID/IQ) contract awarded in September 2004 to the NCI Information Systems Inc.(NCI) team. It provides Network-Centric Information technology, Networking, Telephony and Security, Voice, Video and Data Communications, Commercial-off-the-Shelf (COTS) products, system solutions, engineering services, systems hardware and software, systems management, operations and maintenance support, configuration management, training, centralized logistics and inventory management support. This includes depot level support, spares and/or supplies, to satisfy the requirements for interoperability, compatibility and resource sharing of both Government Furnished Equipment (GFE) and Contractor Furnished Equipment (CFE), supporting the Global Information Grid (GIG) architecture. As part of NCI’s Team, this contract enables Macalogic to provide flexible end-user solutions that embrace diverse current and future network-centric information technology requirements, ensure interoperabili ty through standards-based technology (using both GFE and CFE) and implement the constant evolution of state-of-the-art technology and systems solutions. This BPA can be used by all DoD agencies.
